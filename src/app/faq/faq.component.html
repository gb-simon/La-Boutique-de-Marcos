La Boutique de Marcos is a garage project we started as a joke, Marcos is our Front-End Dev. La Boutique is an e-commerce where you can look for all kinds of products, you can filter by categories, select a product, and put it on the car as you can keep browsing our website!

By using La Boutique you make the world better... and earn IQ. When registering on our website, you're not only having another place to publish your products, you're being part of a whole new market where customers and sellers go first. If is there something that users highlight on our website is the great user experience we offer, that makes transactions faster than the speed of light!

To know more about us you can check on the about section, there you'll find information about the developers and their roles on the project!

Documentation


The Boutique is a project that we have decided to do to get practice in web programming and also to give a point of reference to our team on how experienced we are. This project is made with Angular and Tailwind CSS. It should be noted that Angular is a tool that none of the developers had used before, that is, in a very short time we have managed to internalize with the framework and managed to create this site consisting of pure Front-End.

It consists of 9 pages and 13 components. Among the pages we have included:
