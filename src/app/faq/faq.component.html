Frequently Asked Questions

What is La Boutique? What benefits do i get if i register in the boutique? Who have made La Boutique?

La Boutique is a garage project we started as a joke. It is called "La Boutique de Marcos". Marcos is our Front-End Dev. La Boutique is an e-commerce where you can look for all kinds of products, you can filter by categories, select a product, and put it on the car as you can keep browsing our website!

What benefits do I get if I register in the boutique? By using La Boutique you make the world better... and earn IQ. When registering on our website, you're not only having another place to publish your products, you're being part of a whole new market where customers and sellers go first. If is there something that users highlight on our website is the great user experience we offer, that makes transactions faster than the speed of light!
